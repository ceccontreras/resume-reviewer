Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

My Resume Rate ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign up, your name and email address are managed by Clerk, our authentication provider. We store only a unique identifier linked to your account.
  • Resume content: The text extracted from resumes you upload, along with the job descriptions you provide, are stored to show your analysis history.
  • Usage data: We track the number of analyses you run each month to enforce plan limits.
  • Payment information: If you upgrade to Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide resume analysis powered by Google Gemini AI.
  • To display your analysis history and allow you to manage it.
  • To enforce free-tier usage limits and manage your subscription.
  • To improve the service over time.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Resume and job description text is sent to Google's Gemini API solely to generate analysis results. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle that data.

4. Data Retention

Your analysis history is retained until you delete it or close your account. You can delete individual analyses at any time from the history panel.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication is handled by Clerk, which uses industry-standard security practices.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at ceccontreras5@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

Questions? Reach us at ceccontreras5@gmail.com.
