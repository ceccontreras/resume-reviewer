    Our model uses Google Gemini to analyze alignment between your experience and the job requirements, closely mirroring how Applicant Tracking Systems evaluate candidates.

    Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

    Yes — cancel anytime from your account. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

    What file formats do you support?

    PDF only, up to 10MB. Make sure your resume is text-based (not a scanned image).
